Additional Registration Requirement for Foreign Students

All Foreign Returning and New Students, Diploma, Undergraduateand Post-graduate Students must satisfy
the following registration requirement before they can proceed with their studies in the University:

Possession of the following documents

  1. Admission Letter
  2. International Passport or ECOWAS Travel Certficate
  3. Valid VISA-STR(Subject to Regularization)
  4. Residence Permit

Failure to secure such document will lead to suspension of all academic activities
